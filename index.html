About

"You must be the change you wish to see in the world."

-Mahatma Gandhi

Youth Parliament is a platform offered to the youngsters where they get an opportunity to understand the working of parliament and to acquire or improve upon many skills such as public speaking, debate, presentation and research.In a country hit by deep social crisis,poverty,rising crime,extremism and other issues of national and global concern, the Youth Parliament plays an important role in channelizing the energies of young students towards creativity and helps them to inculcate a sense of leadership qualities

The ' Youth Parliament 2017 ' organized by the Lingua Franca team in JSSATEN campus was huge success which not only captured mass attention but also helped the young leaders to put forward their views and reflect upon matters of social,political as well as other fields of public interest. It was an inter-college event where 150 leaders from various branches met at JSSATEN campus to engage on debatable issues. The parliament included the affirmation,arbitrary references,introduction to ministers,question hour, bill session, attention notice and much more. The young leaders participated with full enthusiasm and exhibited their convincing skills over topics such as demonetization, surgical strike,uniform civil code, etc. The one day event was highly cherised and ended with constructive conclusions . It successfully accomplished the purpose of a Youth Parliament and we look forward for your support and participation in Youth Parliament -2018 in the same manner.

This year, the Lingua Franca team is presenting a two day Youth Parliament where young leaders will come under limelight at JSS periphery .This time it will be a more grand and fascinating event where the two different houses of the parliament ( Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha) will dicuss over public welfare issues. The discussion session will comprise of three sessions namely Zero hour, Question hour and bill proceedings. The participants will be provided an atmosphere where they can actually feel the zeal of a national leader.It will deepen their understanding of parliamentary democracy and will help them to bring out their hidden talents,convincing skills and ideas which will be a great aid in their personality development.

The Youth Parliament-2018 will be held on 17th and 18th February, 2018 in the prestigious campus of JSS Academy of Technical Education.The participants will discuss over contemporary issues or agendas as decided by the core organizers.

The participants will have the liberty to choose English or Hindi as their mode of expression .With a perspective that words and ideas can change the world ,we welcome the youngsters to be a part of this great inter-college event and enhance their knowledge and skills.
